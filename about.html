Get to Know Me

My name is Allie Drake and I am a second year student at Drexel University studying Interactive Digital Media. I'm more designed focus and have channeled those elements into creating content, designing platforms, and developing usable interfaces. I like to be creative and implement the inspiration from the world around me to make an impact through my work.
